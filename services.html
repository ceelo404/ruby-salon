Brandy Claussen Holland: Master Stylist & Colorist

With a distinguished career spanning over two decades, Brandy Claussen Holland is a true artisan in the world of hair. Her journey began with an intensive foundation at the prestigious Vidal Sassoon Academy in London, followed by advanced training with industry leaders Toni & Guy and two sessions at Bumble and Bumble in NYC. This extensive education instilled a mastery of precision cutting and an exceptional eye for detail, making her a trusted expert in her field.

Brandy's passion lies in enhancing natural beauty. She specializes in creating stunning, natural-looking color, with a particular love for lived-in blondes and dimensional brunettes. She also excels at crafting seamless, low-maintenance looks that grow out beautifully. Her expertise extends to all hair types, with a celebrated skill in cutting and styling curly hair of all lengths.

Beyond her technical prowess, Brandy's philosophy is simple: she believes in making every client feel like the best version of themselves. Her warm and welcoming approach ensures a comfortable, confident experience from start to finish. Her dedication to the art of hairstyling has earned her a loyal clientele and a reputation for consistently delivering impeccable results.
